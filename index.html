FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Minswap is the leading decentralized exchange on Cardano, processing about 70% of the chain's DEX trading volume. It's entirely community-driven with no VC backing, and focuses on fair access to liquidity and trading for everyone holding ADA or Cardano tokens.

Connect your Cardano wallet (Eternl, Nami, or Lace), choose the tokens you want to trade, enter the amount, and confirm. The platform settles most swaps quickly with low fees.

MIN is the governance and utility token distributed through a Fair Initial Stake Pool Offering. Earn MIN through yield farming or liquidity provision, and participate in protocol governance decisions.

Stake LP tokens from liquidity provision or MIN directly to earn additional rewards. Over 599 million MIN are currently locked in farming contracts, reflecting strong community participation.

Minswap uses audited Plutus V2 smart contracts on Cardano. All trading is non-custodial. Governance is managed by a DAO LLC structure for transparency and community oversight.

Native Bitcoin is expected to arrive on Cardano via the Cardinal Protocol integration, making BTC-ADA trading pairs possible on Minswap. Once live, users will be able to swap BTC directly.
